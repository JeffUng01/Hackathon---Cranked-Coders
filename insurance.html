How to File Insurance


  • With many people suffering from property damage and needing medical assistance people need to know how to file insurance claims.


  • 1. if the type of situation you have needs to require the police please do so. This is usualy in the case of a breakin or theft

  • 2. Try to document everything that you can, by taking pictures and getting the information you might need to file the claim

  • 3. Contact you insurance company, below we have a list of the claims and support departments for most major insurance companines

    • How to Apply for FEMA AID


  • Below are the instructions for apply for the FEMA desaster Relief Aid, the Website link for FEMA, and the documents needed for applying


  • 1.Account for all damages and lost items with photos

  • 2.Create a list documenting all of the items lost or damaged

  • 3.If you have insurance you are strongly encouraged to apply for a claim with you insurace provide, this may also be faster

  • 4.If you do not have insurace you may apply with the multiple ways below

  • Online Application

  • FEMA PHONE #: 1-800-621-FEMA (3362)

  • In person at your local FEMA Disaster Recovery Center

  • Mail Application to: FEMA P.O. Box 10055 Hyattsville, MD 20782-8055

  • Fax Application to: Attn:FEMA 800-827-8112

  • Documents you need to provide:

  • Insurance determination letter

  • Proof of occupancy or ownership

  • Proof of ID
